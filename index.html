Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions

How does the onboarding process work?

You submit the onboarding form with your business details, our team reviews it and reaches out, and we then build and configure your storefront for you. Most stores are ready within a few days.

Is my store and customer data secure?

Yes. MyMakola is built with modern security best practices, including encrypted data and secure payment handling, so your business and your customers stay protected.

Do I need technical skills to set up my store?

Not at all. Our team handles the technical setup for you, and the admin dashboard is designed so that anyone can manage products and orders without any coding knowledge.

How much does MyMakola cost?

Plans start at GH₵149/month for Starter. The Business plan is GH₵349/month with unlimited products and advanced features, and Enterprise pricing is tailored to your needs. There are no hidden fees.

What kind of support do you offer?

Every plan includes support from a dedicated team. Starter includes email support, Business gets priority support, and Enterprise customers receive 24/7 premium support with a dedicated account manager.

Will my store work well on mobile phones?

Absolutely. Every MyMakola storefront is fully responsive and optimised for mobile, because most shoppers in Ghana browse and buy on their phones.

What payment methods can my customers use?

Your store can securely accept mobile money and card payments, making it easy for customers across Ghana to check out using the method they prefer.

Can I customise the look of my storefront?

Yes. Your store is branded to match your business, and you can update your logo, colours, products and content at any time. Enterprise plans include deeper customisation and integrations.
